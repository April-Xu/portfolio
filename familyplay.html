Overview
Duration: 3 days | Adobe x Activision Creative Jam | Teammates: Yu Zhao, Xin Su
Using Adobe XD, I finished 60%+ of the design work. The processes I participated includes literature review, persona construction, information architecture identification, wireframing and hi-fi prototype design, 2 complete design iterations, interactive prototype design, and formal documentation.
Problem Space
How to build a proficiency-based app to help parents to find age-appropriate games for their children?
Solution
SOLUTION
A personalized professional mobile app that helps parents finding age-appropriate games and monitoring children's growth at the same time
The solution we proposed allows parents to find games according to the specific needs of their children. They can get recommended games or freely explore top-rated games. They can also keep track of children’s playing context and the relevant skill development.
Onboarding experience
Gather device, children, and game-related information to asure professional and personalized recommendation with user control
Game exploration
New-gamer-friendly exploration based on customized recommendation and free discovery with prioritized core information
Growth records
Track children’s growth in the aspect of relevant skills developed and easily plan gaming experience
User Needs
The user needs we intended to address:
Be aware of the games’ basic information - recommended age, platform, content, genre, relevant skills, etc
Understand the gaming context - duration, accompany, environment, etc
Be able to interact beyond the games - communications between family members and/or other families
