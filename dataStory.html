Hi, this is the documentation for my final project.

This is for the Telling Stories with Data class at CMU.

Project outline



This is a project about loneliness epidemic, a mental health issue that's been worse and worse after COVID19 quarantine.

The major elements of my story will be a hook, an introduction of current situation, an explanation about why loneliness is serious, a section of whose feeling of loneliness are mostly affected by COVID19 and how, a section of how to deal with it, and an appendix for acknowledging data sources.

I expect the readers to think about their current lives at first, be aware of the severeness of the situation, and be able to cope with similar situations after reading my story.

I plan to use Figma, Flourish, and Shorthand to design and develop my project. Figma will only be used for possible artifacts I might creat(e.g. some photo editing or some specific visualization) and Shorthand is used for the website. Flourish will be used for conventional or complicated data visualizations. I will use Figma to wireframe my ideas and redesign the projects based on future user research. I will conduct at least 3 user research, gather their feedback, and make revisions until it's polished and ready for presentation.
